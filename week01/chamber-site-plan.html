The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Ogden City, Utah Chamber of Commerce

Reason: The actual local Chamber of Commerce is Ogden-Weber Chamber of Commerce. I don't expect there to be copyright issues in this course but I intentionally chose a different name to avoid any issues. This is a location that is close to my hometown.

Our Mission (Site Purpose)

The Ogden Chamber of Commerce serves to connect you and your business to professionals and citizens within Weber County and throughout all of Utah. We provide resources to network, receive eduation, develop leadership skills, and improve our Utah communities.

Scenarios

Questions

1. What events will be happening?
2. Is this an organization I would want to purchase a membership for?
3. How can I contact the Chamber of Commerce with questions?

The above questions could drive the following content:

• Current Events
• Weather Report
• Company Reviews
• Contact Us
