Membership benefits

Non Profit Membership

  • Cost: No Fees to Join
  • Benefits Include:
    • Access to online training programs
    • Advertising opportunies (e.g. website and newletters)
    • 10% event discounts

Bronze Membership

  • Cost: $300, 10% discount if paid in full annually
  • Benefits Include:
    • Access to online training programs
    • Advertising opportunies (e.g. website and newletters)
    • 15% event discounts
      • Priority registration for events before non-profit

Silver Membership

  • Cost: $350, 10% discount if paid in full annually
  • Benefits Include:
    • Access to online training programs
    • Advertising opportunies (e.g. website and newletters)
    • 20% event discounts
      • Priority registration for events before non-profit and bronze members

Gold Membership

  • Cost: $400, 10% discount if paid in full annually
  • Benefits Include:
    • Access to online training programs
    • Advertising opportunies (e.g. website and newletters)
    • 25% event discounts
      • Priority registration for events before non-profit, bronze, and silver members
